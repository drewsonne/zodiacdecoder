Hello Internet.
The University of North Texas Symbolic Processing class is proud to present the beta release of the ZodiacDecoder.
Our goal is to crack the previously undeciphered 340 character "dripping pen" cipher.
With the help of your CPU cycles, we can find out what this cryptic message says.
Throughout our refinement of this client, we will be keeping you posted on the progress with this blog.

Our current roadmap sees the implementation of:
- Score tweaking
- Voting
- EM algorithm
- graphical niceness
- partying when we decode this motha...

We are currently making use of 3 algorithms to crack the code:
- Genetic
- Intelligent Brute Force
- Random

Much thanks to Prof. Ryan Garlick, and the entire class for coming together and making this happen.
